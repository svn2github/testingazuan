Introduction

The term Business Intelligence is so widely used to risk loosing a proper meaning, because of the vastness and diversity of all the realities it implies. For this reason we believe that first of all it’s useful to define what is the meaning of Business Intelligence and how SpagoBI can support it.

In our opinion it is misleading the usage of the term Business Intelligence in order to indifferently indicate static reports, tools for their development, dimensional analysis, data organization cubes, data warehousing or data mining models, them of all often inserted into the context of a particular product or a particular implementation. It’s also partial and limitative the interpretation of Business Intelligence as a separate collection of some of them.

Business Intelligence identifies a solution suitable for the building of a coherent representation of one’s own business. By means of a direct and effective coordination of different fragmented applications it is accessible from more points of view and with different degrees of rights. This happens using all the tools listed before, organically inserted into all the neuralgic cores of a particular business field.

So we aren’t talking about a software development closed into a specific product’s boundaries or into a particular realization (that often means the usage of a predefined software package), but we are talking about a solution able to harmonically integrate:

  • the strategic vision for the own business and the relative functional competence;
  • some specific tools (Data Warehousing, OLAP, Data Mining , Dashboard, ETL);
  • a technological and applicative infrastructure allowing a fluent integration for the single modules.
SpagoBI is the technological and applicative infrastructure using the most interesting existing Free Open Source Solutions (as reporting engines, OLAP, Data Mining); it can be integrated with different proprietary solutions too. In this way, it establishes the technological and applicative precondition for the development of a Business Intelligence solution inside a particular software project: in fact, can not set aside the strategic vision your own business and its consequent functional competence.
